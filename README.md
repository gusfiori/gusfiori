### Research

**PIBIC/CNPq — Adaptive Regularized Ensemble in Imbalanced Data Streams** *(2024–2025)*
Evaluated the ARE algorithm against 8 ensemble competitors across datasets with up to 500k instances and imbalance ratios up to 99:1. ARE achieved the best predictive ranking (Kappa index) on real-world datasets while maintaining significantly lower computational cost than complex ensembles like ARF and SRP.
